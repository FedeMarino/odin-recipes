Caesar Salad

Description

Caesar salad is a green salad that became a true classic and a staple item on restaurant menus.

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce
  • Shredded parmesan cheese (to taste)
  • 1/2 a baguette for the croutons
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 clove of minced garlic
  • salt, pepper, dijon, worcestershire sauce, lemon juice and red wine vinegar (to taste)

Steps

How to make the croutons

  1. Cut baguette in half lengthwise and slice into 1/4â€³ thick pieces and place on baking sheet.
  2. Combine 3 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil with minced garlic. toss breads with garlic infused oil and 2 Tbsp parmesan.
  3. Spread Evenly and Bake to desired crispness.

How to make Caesar dressing

  1. Whisk together minced garlic, dijon, Worcestershire, lemon juice and red wine vinegar.
  2. Whisking while adding oil emulsifies the dressing for a smooth and creamy (not oily) consistency.
  3. Season with 1/2 tsp salt and 1/8 tsp black pepper, or to taste.

Preparation

In a large mixing bowl, combine all of your ingredients and toss gently to coat the lettuce in caesar dressing.
